About JCS for Elasticsearch

JCS for Elasticsearch (ES for short) is a distributed full-text search service based on the open-source Elasticsearch. This product provides the high-availability, easy-expandability and near-real time search capacity, devotes to mass data storage and search and real-time log analysis, and aims to provide more stable and more reliable cloud search service to users depending on compatible opening, flexible elasticity and safety and controllability.
